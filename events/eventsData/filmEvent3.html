Children's Film Fest (Mar 31)

Info:

Fun for the whole family to enjoy! Presenting a list of films that famous world around, including classics and new hits, but what's really the icing on the cake are the three short films made and produced by students from the local school districts. Jay Peters, founder of the event and La Jolla resident, wanted to find a way to give back to the community and remind people of the classic films that he and others adored when they were children. The new films draw in the crowds, and the older films are a wonderful discovery for folks of all ages. Jay really wanted to buid his community, however, leading him to also include a film competition for elementary school, junior high school, and high school. He hopes this incentive for filmmaking will encourage youth to pursue a dream he wish he could have pursued as a child. Come bring the family and expect to be wow'd!
