La Jolla Symphony Presents Star Wars (Jan 7 - Jan 21)

Info:

The La Jolla Symphony is often known for its music off the beaten path; Berlioz, Reich, and Cage for example are part of their common repetoire, as well as the classics of course from Romance and Baroque. That's why it's catching our attention that they'll be playing for the silver screen! For three weeks in a row, they will perform the Star Wars trilogy stories! This is a once in a lifetime opportunity. Watching the film with a live orchestra takes that galaxy far far away and brings it into your local culture-sphere. Including special guests!
