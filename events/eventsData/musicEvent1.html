Kaboo Del Mar

Info:

When Del Mar became well recognized for its beautiful beaches and famous race track, several young faces were bound to come, interested in what North County had to offer. Among their interests they brought a sincere desire to learn about musicians and popular artists. Enter Sam Langdon, who when he first laid eyes on the race track, exclaimed "How can we not invite the world to perfect concert grounds like these?!?" Langdon pursued the idea further, and in 2010 landed a gig with local musicians, performers, and comedians. These days, it has grown to accomodate world renowned artists, yet it is still a local favorite to attend each year.
