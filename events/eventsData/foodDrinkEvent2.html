The Quintessential Festival (Apr 28)

Info:

The Quintessential Festival is one of San Diego's more best kept secret beer festivals. While other events tend to be overcrowded with tourists who don't know their limits, the true local experience is found here. Created and hosted by the La Jolla Golden Triangle Rotary Club, several members work their connections to bring only the best to our enjoyment. Along with San Diego classics such as AleSmith, Ballast Point, and Saint Archer, discover other unique treasurers from vendors visiting from across the border, such as Boca Roja winery. The whole event is accented with cornhole, live music, restaurant vendors, the knowledge that your donations will go straight back into the community, aiding this Rotary club in holding events for local service and international service causes, including homelessness, environment, and educational initiatives. Please drink responsibly, ages 21+.
