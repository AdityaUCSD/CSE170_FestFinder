USD Torero Dance Team

BIOGRAPHY:

It's odd that despite ranking fourth in nationals, this talented dance team is not well known across the county. The Torero dance team, despite being new to the University of San Diego, is setting a name for itself, and you should see what they're about. In a unique combination of culture and ballet, these dancers learn how to work as team and how to present a message to their audience. Because they are still new, most of their performances are free, with any proceeds donated to the Uptown Community. See where they will be performing now!
