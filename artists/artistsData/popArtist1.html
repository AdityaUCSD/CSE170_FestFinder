Popstar French

BIOGRAPHY:

That's right, you heard correctly! Popstar began in middle school making covers of famous pop songs. What really caught the attention of her peers and fans was that she would connect each song to a motivating message that built people up. It didn't take long before the talent bubbled up within her to write songs herself to motivate and inspire others. Her smile says it all, she is a peace maker wherever she goes. She is starting to tour the county for now, and although she is sometimes nervous to perform in front of live audiences, attending an event where she will sing is an experience you cannot miss.
