Sally McDonald

BIOGRAPHY:

Sally takes a new perspective with country singing. Having lived all her life in the county, you might not expect country to be her thing, but she fell in love with it. Her parents played it all the time in the car and on the radio. Her first concert was VIP passes to Stagecoach out in Indio/Coachella Valley. What does she sing about? The same thing as any other country singer: the open skies, caring for her animals, and all the local San Diego beers. That's what makes her unique, that's what makes San Diego love her.
