KunFusion

BIOGRAPHY:

KunFusion began not long ago, in 2012, as a new form of dance style that caught popularity. However, KunFusion really ups the game every time they perform! Their moves always seem more intense, passionate, and well coordinated. Justin Ho, a third year UCSD undergraduate who is captain of the new recruits, explains that their skill is thanks to a strong sense of unity and family, which would not be possible if it weren't for the local entertainment they have enjoyed in the past together. For that reason, they are giving back to the community by performing and encouraging dance and self worth.
